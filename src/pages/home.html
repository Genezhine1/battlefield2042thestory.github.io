WELCOME TO THE BATTLEFIELD 2042 SUMMARY

The method to tell a narrative in this game is unique for the franchise and can put some people off at first glance on its massive databases of lore. This summary aims to collect essential information from all of these databases such as the Codex, web lore, playercards and more to tell a cohesive and streamlined story.

This site will continue to receive content and updates so long as Battlefield 2042's narrative is ongoing.

NAVIGATION:

To navigate the site simple hover over and press any of the buttons at the top of the page. The buttons are in chronological order starting from the left. To return to the homepage, press the "back to homepage" button at the bottom of the page.

Highlighted words can be hovered over to reveal more information.
