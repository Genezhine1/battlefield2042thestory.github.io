WELCOME TO THE BATTLEFIELD 2042 SUMMARY

The method to tell a narrative in this game is unique for the franchise and can be difficult for some people to navigate. This summary aims to collect essential information from the Codex, web lore, playercards and more to tell the main story that is laid out.

This site will continue to receive content and updates as long as Battlefield 2042's narrative is ongoing.

NAVIGATION:

To navigate the site simple hover over and press any of the buttons at the top of the page. The buttons are in chronological order starting from the left. Refresh the site to return to the homepage.
